## ⟡ About

I'm a final-year **B.Tech Artificial Intelligence & Data Science** student at Christ The King Engineering College (Anna University), India, currently working as an AI & Data Science Researcher at JODHRS International. My work sits at the intersection of **medical imaging AI**, **LLM security**, and **multi-agent systems** — with a research background in Biology & Mathematics that shapes my focus on clinically-grounded AI.

I'm building toward a research-oriented MSc in Artificial Intelligence in Germany, with the long-term goal of working on foundation models and trustworthy AI systems that hold up under real-world, adversarial conditions.
